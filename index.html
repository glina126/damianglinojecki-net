MicroBit 328

Arduinos these days take various shapes and sizes. I looked at everything available in the market, and they all had one thing in common; 2.54mm header spacing. This bothered me as it was very often uncessary for such thick wires. It also made the Arduinos big, bulky and actually pricey due to their size. I looked at the available components and set out to build the smallest, yet just as powerful of an Arduino as the UNO. It has became my goal, and this goal is almost there, almost.
